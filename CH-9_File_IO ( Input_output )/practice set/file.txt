😄 Yeh lo — Donkey Summary in 10 lines:

Donkey ek domestic animal hai jo hazaron saalon se insaan ka kaam aata hai.
Iska scientific naam Equus asinus hai — horse ka chhota bhai samjho.
Donkey bahut stubborn (ziddi) hote hain — ye actually self-protection instinct hai, bewakoofi nahi!
Inki yaaddasht bahut tez hoti hai — 25 saal pehle mili jagah bhi yaad rehti hai.
Donkey akele darne lagte hain — hamesha group mein ya kisi dost ke saath khush rehte hain.
Inki awaaz ko "bray" kehte hain — "Hee-Haw" jo 3 km door tak sunai deta hai!
Donkey ka lifespan 25-40 saal hota hai — kafi lamba jeete hain.
Ye bahut strong hote hain — apne weight ka 20% tak bojh utha sakte hain.
Duniya mein lagbhag 40 million donkeys hain — mostly Asia aur Africa mein.
Donkey ko gadhaa kehte hain lekin ye actually bahut intelligent aur loyal animals hote hain! 🫏
